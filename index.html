Fight a Monster, Get Its Card

Every monster with a respective card can drop that card, or a shiny/alt art version if you're lucky! Simply farm for the card just like you would any other drop. Regular cards drop at a rate of 1/100 and foil/alt cards drop at a rate of 1/250.

Fight Repeatable Bosses, Get Packs

In addition to their card, repeatable bosses can drop card packs. Packs are limited to cards in their set - at launch there is a Gielinor set and a Lumbridge-Draynor set. Packs are important, as they are the only way to obtain the many cards not associated with killable monsters! Card packs drop at a rate of 1/100. Note that card packs and cards share a drop table, so if one of them drops you won't be getting the other.

Initial Roadmap

  • Alpha: Card balancing where cards can be fully reworked, removed, or added. Game mechanics subject to change, but less likely. Continual development of RuneLite plugin features.
  • Beta: Card balancing, mostly stat tweaks. Continual development of RuneLite plugin features.
  • 1.0: Gielinor set and Lumbridge-Draynor set cards will no longer recieve changes except in extreme circumstances. Continual development of RuneLite plugin features.
